- About Us -

Agritourism is defined more broadly, involves any agriculture-based operation or activity that brings visitors to a farm or ranch. Agritourism has different definitions in different parts of the world, and sometimes refers specifically to farm stays , Agritourism includes a wide variety of activities including buying produce directly from a farm stand, navigating in the orchid plantation, corn fields and horticulture fields, picking fruits and vegetables and animals. Agritourism is a form of niche tourism that is considered a growth industry in parts of the world, with value add products, farm direct marketing and sustainable agriculture.

About Agrowvillage

There is more lesson to be learnt from soil than from classroom. No teacher better than nature can teach a child the lesson of discipline, hard work, timeline, patience, judgment, risk management, resource optimization and profitability. Agrowvillage has been conceptualized to bring people close to the nature through exposure to farming and agriculture. The dedicated team of nature lovers with knowledge and expertise in farming and, agriculture arrange field trips for children. Children get a much require break from the world of mobile, TV and other gadgets to indulge themselves in the bliss of greenery. They get an opportunity to try their hands-on farming and are introduced to basic concepts, techniques and technology related to agriculture.

Come, indulge in pure soil and reap he riches of nature's blessings.
